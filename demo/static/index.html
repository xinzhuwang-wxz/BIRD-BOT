One backyard feeder. Three points of view.

BirdBot is the cloud AI layer that turns a smart feeder's photos, on-device detections, time and place into a living nature story — what bird is this, what's it doing, why now, is it a regular, which frame to keep, what happened today. This showcase runs the real recognition, rarity, governance and chat components end-to-end, offline. Pick a viewpoint.
